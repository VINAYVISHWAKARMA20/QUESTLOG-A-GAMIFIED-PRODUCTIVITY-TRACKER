## 🌌 Welcome to the Space Odyssey
**QuestLog** is not just another to-do list. It is a fully gamified, RPG-style productivity tracker that turns your daily habits and tasks into an epic interstellar adventure. 

Instead of just checking off boxes, you earn EXP, level up your spaceship, and physically travel across a massive 2.5D **Cosmic Journey Map** to conquer 7 unique high-fidelity planets. But beware—miss your tasks, and your ship takes Hull Damage (HP). Run out of HP, and the consequences are brutal!

---

## ✨ Features in the release version 

### 🛠️ Daily & Recurring Quests
- Create **Habits** and **One-off Tasks** easily.
- Support for **Recurring Tasks** (Daily, Weekly, Monthly) that automatically duplicate upon completion.
- **Task Sorting & Management:** Keep your daily objectives organized with smart sorting categories.
- **Ringing & Notifications:** Set active alarms and reminders so you never forget to complete a quest.
- Satisfying complete animations with haptic feedback and custom sound alerts.

### 🎮 The RPG System
- **EXP & Levels:** Completing tasks grants EXP. Level up to travel further into the universe.
- **HP (Health Points):** You are penalized for missing deadlines! If your HP hits zero, you suffer an RPG death penalty: you lose a full level, 50% of your current XP, and your activity streak resets.
- **Critical Success Drops:** Sometimes completing a task grants 3x EXP and rare **Cosmic Dust**.
- **Unified Neon HUD:** Track your HP, XP, and Level directly from a glowing tactical dash across the app.

### 🪐 The Cosmic Journey Map
- **7 Unique High-Fidelity Planets:** Travel past massive, completely custom shader-rendered planets:
  - *Spark* (A blazing sun)
  - *Habit Genesis* (A Martian-cratered world)
  - *Focus Sphere* & *Routine Core* (Massive Gas Giants with animated rings)
  - *Consistency Ridge* (Neon-cyan Ice World)
  - *Mastery Prime* (A sleek, minimal dark core singularity)
- Infinite, mathematically accurate auto-scrolling space travel matching your real-life level progression.
- Beautiful, fully animated background starfields and shooting comets dynamically drawn behind every screen in the app.

### 🏪 Intergalactic Shop & Pets
Spend your gathered **Cosmic Dust** in the Shop:
- **Consumables:** Buy Health Potions or EXP Tomes.
- **Gacha Gambling:** Buy a Meteor Shower for a randomized drop (Potions, Dust, rare EXP Stars).
- **Utility Items:** Buy Deflector Shields to block incoming HP damage, or Station Docking Passes to pause your streak during vacations!
- **Cosmic Pets & Ship Colors:** Adopt pets (like the highly coveted animated Cosmic Dragon!) that physically fly alongside your spaceship on the map!
- **Titles:** Buy bragging-rights Titles that show below your level.

## 💻 Tech Stack
- **Platform:** Android studio (Java)
- **UI System:** XML (Material Design Components + Custom Canvas Graphics)
- **Graphics Engine:** Fully custom software rendering using `Canvas`, `RadialGradient`, `Path`, and complex `Shader` sweeps for stunning UI effects without heavy Unity/OpenGL overhead.
- **Local Database:** SQLite via `DatabaseHelper`
